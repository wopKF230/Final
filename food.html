Reganmian

Reganmian, or hot dry noodles, is one of the most famous dishes in Wuhan. This delicious dish features noodles tossed in a sesame paste-based sauce, with toppings like pickled vegetables, scallions, and chili oil, creating a perfect blend of flavors.

Doupi

Doupi is a traditional Wuhan breakfast dish made with glutinous rice, minced pork, mushrooms, and a crispy bean-curd sheet. It's pan-fried to golden perfection and loved for its crispy outer layer and savory filling.
